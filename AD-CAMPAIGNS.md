## Campaign Strategy Overview

Southern Ground Capital operates in the competitive private lending space where speed, trust, and deal certainty are the three decisive factors for real estate investors choosing a lender. The ad strategy should center on **speed-to-close** as the primary differentiator (24-48 hour approvals, 5-10 day funding) while using **social proof** ($500M+ funded, 15+ years) to build instant credibility against fly-by-night lenders.

The funnel is short and high-intent: real estate investors actively searching for capital have a deal in hand and need funding now. Google Search captures bottom-of-funnel demand (investors Googling "hard money lender near me"). Meta and YouTube build awareness with investors who aren't actively searching but are in the market for their next deal. LinkedIn targets commercial investors and referral partners (brokers, agents, wholesalers).

The retargeting strategy focuses heavily on the consideration-to-conversion gap: investors who visited the site and used the loan calculator but didn't submit a quote request. These warm leads need trust reinforcement (testimonials, funded deal examples) and urgency (competitive deals don't wait for slow capital).
